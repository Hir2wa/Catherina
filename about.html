About Rwanda

Discover the heart of Africa - The Land of a Thousand Hills

Cultural Heritage

Rwanda's rich cultural heritage is deeply rooted in its traditions, arts, and community values.

Traditional Customs

  • Intore Dance - traditional Rwandan dance
  • Ubwoko - clan system and heritage
  • Agaciro - dignity and self-respect
  • Umuganda - community service tradition

Arts & Crafts

  • Imigongo - geometric art designs
  • Traditional basketry (Agaseke)
  • Pottery and ceramics
  • Contemporary art galleries in Kigali

Language & Communication

  • Kinyarwanda - national language
  • English and French widely used
  • Traditional storytelling
  • Respectful greeting customs

Wildlife & Nature

Rwanda is home to diverse wildlife species and unique ecosystems.

Primates

  • Mountain Gorillas
  • Golden Monkeys
  • Chimpanzees
  • Colobus Monkeys

Big Game

  • African Elephants
  • Lions
  • Leopards
  • Cape Buffalo

Birds

  • Grey Crowned Cranes
  • Great Blue Turacos
  • Ross's Turacos
  • Various raptor species

Getting Around

Public Transport

  • Public buses between cities
  • Shared taxis (matatus)
  • Motorcycle taxis (motos)
  • City buses in Kigali

Private Transport

  • Car rental services
  • Private drivers
  • Organized tours
  • Airport transfers

Climate Information

Rwanda enjoys a temperate tropical highland climate with year-round mild temperatures.

Season Months Temperature Rainfall
Long Dry Season June - September 25°C - 27°C Low
Short Dry Season December - February 23°C - 25°C Moderate
Long Rainy Season March - May 20°C - 22°C High
Short Rainy Season October - November 22°C - 24°C Moderate
