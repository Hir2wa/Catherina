Our Camping Destinations

Discover Rwanda's most beautiful locations for your next adventure

Volcanoes National Park

Experience the majesty of Rwanda's mountain gorillas in their natural habitat, surrounded by the stunning Virunga Mountains.

Location Details

Located in Kinigi, Musanze District, Northern Province. The park is a 2-hour drive from Kigali and borders both Congo and Uganda.

What to Expect

High-altitude environment with temperatures ranging from 11°C to 21°C. Expect humid conditions and occasional rain. Professional guides accompany all activities.

Activities Available

  • Mountain gorilla tracking
  • Golden monkey visits
  • Dian Fossey's grave hike
  • Mount Karisimbi climbing
  • Cultural village visits

Akagera National Park

Experience Rwanda's only Big Five safari park, filled with stunning savannah landscapes and diverse wildlife.

Location Details

Located in the eastern part of Rwanda, about a 2.5-hour drive from Kigali. The park covers 1,200 square kilometers of savannah and wetland.

What to Expect

Warm climate with temperatures ranging from 18°C to 30°C. A great destination for wildlife safaris, boat tours, and camping.

Activities Available

  • Big Five safari (lions, elephants, leopards, rhinos, buffaloes)
  • Lake Ihema boat tours
  • Bird watching
  • Game drives
  • Camping under the stars

Nyungwe National Park

Explore one of Africa's oldest rainforests, home to diverse wildlife and the spectacular canopy walkway.

Location Details

Situated in southwestern Rwanda, Nyungwe is about a 5-hour drive from Kigali. The park covers over 1000 square kilometers of rainforest.

What to Expect

Tropical rainforest climate with moderate temperatures. Best visited during dry seasons (June-September or December-February).

Activities Available

  • Canopy walkway experience
  • Chimpanzee tracking
  • Waterfall hikes
  • Bird watching tours
  • Forest camping

Lake Kivu

Relax by the tranquil waters of Lake Kivu, perfect for a lakeside retreat, water sports, and exploration of Rwanda's beautiful beaches.

Location Details

Located in the western part of Rwanda, Lake Kivu is a large freshwater lake on the border with the Democratic Republic of Congo.

What to Expect

Moderate climate with temperatures ranging from 20°C to 30°C. Ideal for water activities, relaxation, and lakeside camping.

Activities Available

  • Water sports (boating, kayaking)
  • Coffee and tea plantation tours
  • Lakeside camping
  • Beach relaxation
  • Napoleon Island visits

Kigali City

Explore Rwanda's vibrant capital, home to a rich history, cultural landmarks, and scenic views.

Location Details

Kigali is centrally located, easily accessible from anywhere in Rwanda. It's a 2-hour drive from Nyungwe National Park and just 2.5 hours from Akagera.

What to Expect

Cool, moderate climate with temperatures ranging from 18°C to 27°C. Kigali is known for its cleanliness, modern architecture, and rich culture.

Activities Available

  • Kigali Genocide Memorial
  • Presidential Palace Museum
  • Local craft markets
  • Contemporary art galleries
  • Mount Kigali hike

Gishwati-Mukura National Park

Discover Rwanda's newest national park, filled with rare wildlife, lush forests, and a perfect spot for eco-tourism.

Location Details

Located in the western region, about a 4-hour drive from Kigali, the park offers pristine forest experiences and is Rwanda’s smallest national park.

What to Expect

Cool climate with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 24°C. The park is perfect for forest hikes, bird watching, and chimpanzee tracking.

Activities Available

  • Chimpanzee tracking
  • Monkey watching
  • Forest hiking
  • Bird watching
  • Cultural experiences

Butare (Huye)

Delve into Rwanda's cultural heart, with its historical landmarks and vibrant academic atmosphere.

Location Details

Butare is located in the southern part of Rwanda, approximately 3 hours south of Kigali. The town is home to the National Museum of Rwanda.

What to Expect

The climate is temperate, with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 25°C. Butare is known for its educational institutions and rich cultural heritage.

Activities Available

  • National Museum of Rwanda
  • King’s Palace Museum in Nyanza
  • Traditional dance performances
  • Exploring cultural heritage sites
  • Walking tours around the university town
